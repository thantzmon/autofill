How to use this plugin:

1. Save the directory somewhere on your computer. 
2. On Chrome click the top right button > more tools > extentions
3. Click "load unpacked"
4. Select this folder from your computer
5. Right click the extension logo and click "Options"  
6. Fill in list of text shortcuts you want 
7. You may or may want to make it so you have to turn on this plugin when you go to a page rather than having it be constantly on. To do this right click the extension logo and click "This Can Read and Change Site Data" and click which option you want.
